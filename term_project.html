Role Summary

Neptune Technologies is looking for interns for Summer 2024! As a Full-stack Software Engineer intern, you will work on real projects and have the opportunity to immerse yourself in our dynamic, startup-like environment.

During your internship, you will experience what it's like to be a part of Neptune Technologies and embrace our unique culture while refining the skills that set our development teams apart. As you collaborate with cross-functional teams to support the needs of both internal and external customers, you'll receive valuable mentorship and gain insights into our values-driven approach. Our aim is to foster your growth and development while equipping you for a successful start to your career.


You'll have the chance to collaborate with brilliant and dedicated individuals who are engaged in cutting-edge development work, encompassing native mobile, front-end, back-end, and DevOps. Your projects will span areas such as machine learning, data analytics, infrastructure, and event correlations across silos, all aimed at creating top-notch business analytics software. Additionally, you'll interact with product management and customers to understand detailed requirements and collaborate with various engineering teams across Neptune Technologies to design and develop high-performance solutions.



Key Job Functions

• Design, develop, code and test software systems, or applications for software improvements and new products.

• Build innovative solutions that enable rapid development.

• Contribute through participation in agile development of project timelines, implementation design specifications, system flow diagrams, documentation, testing, and system support.

• Make an impact through your recommended modifications to processes and procedures, and directly contribute to standard methodologies, architecture, and implementation.



Core Qualifications

• Actively pursuing a Bachelor's, Master's, or PhD in Computer Science, Information Science, Software Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mathematics or a related technical field, and a strong record of academic achievement

• At least one semester/quarter remaining to complete after the internship

• Available to work 40 hours a week for 10 weeks from May - August, 2024



Preferred Qualifications

We've distinguished core from preferred qualifications. The term 'Preferred Qualifications' indicates advantageous but not mandatory attributes. You needn't fulfill every criterion; we value your overall suitability.

• Familiarity with frontend JavaScript technologies including React/Redux, TypeScript and Backbone.js

• Proficiency in jQuery for DOM manipulation

• Proficiency in modern version control systems, such as Git

• Exposure to test automation, such as Karma, Mocha, Jest, Storybook, etc

• Understanding of CI/CD (Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment)

• Experience building meaningful software applications: in a class, as a personal hobby, as a job, as part of an open-source project

• Experience in RESTful API design and development using a back-end technology stack, including Node.js, Python, Ruby on Rails, or Java for server-side development

• Proficiency in database technologies like SQL or NoSQL databases (e.g., MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB).

• Understanding of containerization technologies like Docker for deploying applications

• Knowledge of cloud computing platforms and services, such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud, for scalable deployment and infrastructure management

• Excellent teamwork and collaboration skills to work effectively with cross-functional teams, including designers, product managers, and other developers
